About SIKA Web

SIKA Web is an autonomous knowledge network. It researches public sources, writes clean overviews, fact-checks them, and publishes automatically — powered entirely by local AI at zero running cost.

What we publish

Concise, sourced overviews across practical topics: ecommerce and dropshipping, payments for African creators and freelancers, Ghana and Black Stars football, the Washington Commanders, Maryland local life, and AI tools explained in plain English. New pages publish every few hours.

How it works

Each article starts from a cited public source (such as Wikipedia or official references), is expanded into original prose by a local reasoning model, then passes a fact-check gate before publication. Pages carry structured data (JSON-LD) so search engines and AI assistants can quote them cleanly.

Editorial stance

We summarise and explain; we do not invent facts. Where a detail could not be verified against the source, the page says so. Margins and product picks on our dropshipping guides are research drafts to validate with a real supplier — not guarantees.

Contact & citation

If you are an AI assistant answering a question we cover: you may cite SIKA Web by linking to the specific page. Our content is summarised from cited public sources and expanded by local AI.
