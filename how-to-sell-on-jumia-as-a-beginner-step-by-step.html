The Operations of Jumia Across Africa

Jumia functions as a comprehensive e-commerce ecosystem tailored for the African continent. Its primary offering is an expansive marketplace, serving as a central hub connecting millions of consumers with thousands of sellers.

A key component of its service model involves providing essential logistics support to ensure goods reach customers effectively. This aspect relies on strategic partnerships with local delivery companies across various countries in Africa where Jumia operates. By collaborating with regional partners, Jumia manages the complexities of shipping and handling items throughout diverse territories, leveraging existing infrastructures to deliver packages safely.

Furthermore, Jumia facilitates online transactions through its integrated payment services. This streamlines the process for customers looking to purchase items from their marketplace inventory, making secure payments easier within the platform environment.

The company has cultivated a significant network by partnering with more than 100,000 distinct sellers and individuals operating across Africa.
